Paneer Tikka Masala

Description

Welcome to the ultimate guide to making Paneer Tikka Masala, a rich and flavorful vegetarian dish that's beloved in Indian cuisine. This recipe brings together marinated paneer cubes grilled to perfection and then simmered in a creamy, spiced tomato-based gravy. Whether you're new to Indian cooking or a seasoned home chef, this step-by-step recipe ensures you'll create a restaurant-quality meal at home.

The soft paneer absorbs the smoky flavors from the tandoor or grill, while the masala is a perfect balance of tangy tomatoes, aromatic spices, and a touch of creaminess. Ideal for serving with naan, rice, or roti, this dish is perfect for a cozy family dinner or special occasion.

Explore the world of Indian spices and enjoy the bold, comforting flavors of this iconic dish. Let’s get started on making your own mouth-watering Paneer Tikka Masala!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 pound paneer, cut into 1/2-inche cubes
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 1 medium green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 medium jalapeno peppers, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ground cashews
  • 1 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 pint half-and-half
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste

Steps

  1. Add butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add paneer.
  3. Cook and stir until golden, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add onions, bell pepper, jalapeños, ground cashews, garlic paste, ginger paste, cayenne pepper, cumin, coriander, and garam masala.
  5. Cook and stir until well combined and fragrant, about 1 minute.
  6. Mix tomato sauce, half-and-half, and salt into paneer mixture.
  7. simmer until thickened, about 30 minutes
